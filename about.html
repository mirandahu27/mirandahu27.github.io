I am Miranda Hu

Hi there! My name is Miranda Hu, and I am currently (as of writing this article) a second year student at Queen's University, in the Geography and Earth Systems Science program. This website was created as an assignment submission for GPHY229 - Place, Space, Culture Social Life. My website was designed to outline the topics relating to prison and heteronormative spaces, and discusses many aspects about gender stereotypes, suppression, and awareness in many different environments (specifically within prisons). As you read through the different articles of the website, I hope you are able to develop a further understanding of gender identity and sexuality, much like I did during my research process.
